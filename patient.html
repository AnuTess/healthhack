You might be pre-diabetic
Elevated HbA1c levels mean that blood sugar levels have been too high for the past three months (the lifespan of a red blood cell). This test can be used to indicate diabetes or pre-diabetes. While the blood glucose level is the concentration of glucose in your blood at a single point in time (the very moment of the test), the HbA1c test serves as an overall marker of what your average levels are over a period of three months. The higher the HbA1c, the greater the risk of developing or worsening diabetes-related complications.
